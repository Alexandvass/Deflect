# DEFLECT

**A fast, reflex-driven survival game that runs in any browser.**

▶️ **[Play it here](https://deflectg.github.io/Deflect/)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; works on phone and desktop, no install.

---

You're a single ship in a hostile arena, and you don't have a gun. All you have is movement and a dash — a split-second burst that flicks you through danger and, timed right, punches straight through a boss. Everything else is just you, your reflexes, and the swarm closing in.

Bolts streak in from every side, seekers hunt you down, chargers lunge, and bombers detonate into rings of fire — and the deeper you go, the faster and meaner it gets. Scoop up energy to build your multiplier, grab meds to stay alive, and dash through the chaos to come out the other side.

## Features

- **Two modes** — a 7-world, 210-level **Campaign** and an endless **Infinite** mode with a global online leaderboard.
- **Bosses every 10 levels**, with each world capped by a massive final boss — beaten the only way you can, by dashing right through it.
- **Eight enemy types** that each hunt you differently, plus rotating boss attack patterns.
- **Progression** — spend coins on upgrades, unlock ship skins that grant small buffs, and complete optional per-level challenges for bonus rewards.
- **Cloud accounts** so your progress follows you to any device, plus a **Rebirth** system once you've cleared it all.
- **Living, animated worlds** — drifting nebulae, parallax stars, and bosses whose eyes track your every move.

## How to play

| Action | Keyboard | Touch |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Move | WASD / Arrow keys | Drag on the play area |
| Dash | Space / Shift | DASH button |

- **Goal:** survive each wave — grab energy, dodge everything, clear the screen to advance.
- **Dash** gives a split-second of invincibility; use it to slip through bolts and to damage bosses.
- **Bosses:** you have no weapon — *dash through them* to deal damage. Touching them any other way costs a heart.
