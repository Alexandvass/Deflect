Fly, dash, and survive. Dash through bolts to slip past — and dash through bosses to break them.

HOW TO PLAY

GOAL

Survive each wave: grab energy, dodge everything, and clear the screen to move on. Three hits and you're down.

MOVE

Keyboard: WASD or the arrow keys. Phone: drag anywhere on the play area to steer your ship.

DASH

Press Space / Shift (or the DASH button) for a quick burst of speed with a split-second of invincibility — use it to slip through bolts. It recharges on a short cooldown (the bar under your score), and grabbing energy speeds it up.

PICKUPS

Energy = score & coins (chain them for a bigger multiplier). Med = restores a heart.

ENEMIES

bolt (straight) seeker (homes in) rusher (fast) weaver (zig-zags) charger (telegraphs, then lunges) splitter (bursts apart) orbiter (circles you) bomber (explodes into a ring)

BOSSES

You have no weapon — dash THROUGH a boss to damage it. Touching it any other way costs a heart. A huge final boss guards level 30 of every world.

MODES

Campaign = levels & worlds to clear. Infinite = endless waves, and the only mode on the global leaderboard.

SPEND COINS

Upgrades (reset each world, pricier as you go), Cosmetics (kept forever, each gives a small buff), and unlocking each world's level-30 final boss.

CHALLENGES

Most levels show an optional challenge (like "no getting hit") — finish it for bonus coins and a ★.

LOG IN

Log in to keep your progress and play on any device. This is a casual game login — please use a throwaway password, not a real one.
